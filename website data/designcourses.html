Design Courses

The College is an internationally connected creative community of diverse disciplines housed under one roof. We shape our students' futures, preparing them to shape the world through applied knowledge and creativity.

About Our Creative Community

Our art and design courses don’t just train you—they promote alternative ways of thinking, making, and communicating. We provide space, tools, and inspiration to develop your creative practice and clear career paths.

You’ll develop your creative practice while interacting with peers in well-equipped studios, making and digital workshops. Learn professional skills through applied briefs facilitated by our connections with commercial clients, cultural institutions, businesses, and organizations.

Why Study Design at Cantor College?

Innovative Thinking

We encourage alternative approaches to making, thinking, and communicating.

World-Class Facilities

Access state-of-the-art workshops, digital labs, and collaborative studio spaces.

Industry Connections

Work on real-world briefs with commercial clients and cultural organizations.
