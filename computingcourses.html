Computing Courses

Discover cutting-edge courses designed to prepare you for a career in Computing and Technology.

Cantor College offers a range of courses to suit applicants with varying backgrounds and educational abilities. At the undergraduate level, there are single BSc Honours Degree courses in Computing, Computer Networks, Software Engineering, and Cyber Security with Forensics, among others.

Our undergraduate and postgraduate courses span a wide range of specialisms, including Computer Science, Software Development, Information Systems, Networking, and Software Engineering. Be part of a passionate computing community with active student societies in games development, digital forensics, and programming.

All courses are accredited by the British Computer Society and highly relevant to modern industry. Designed to prepare students for professional occupations in Computing and related fields, many graduates also pursue MSc or PhD degrees.

Undergraduate Courses

BSc Computing

Comprehensive coverage of computing fundamentals, including programming, systems analysis, and software engineering.

BSc Cyber Security

Specialize in cybersecurity and digital forensics to prepare for an exciting and growing field.

BSc Computer Networks

Focus on the design, implementation, and management of computer networks and systems.

BSc Software Engineering

Learn advanced techniques in software design, development, and testing for modern applications.
