About Us

Cantor College was established in 1989 to specialize in Computing and Design. At Cantor College, we aim to provide students with the education they need to achieve their career goals, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and experience to succeed. Our college offers opportunities that can give you the edge when entering the workforce, through our teaching and our connections to some of the world's leading researchers and employers. Our students have gone on to successful careers in a wide range of industries across the globe. Whatever your ambitions, our learning and support can help you make the most of your time at Cantor College, both as a student and in your future career.
