Access knowledge, resources, and tools to help you succeed in your academic journey

Welcome to Cantor College Library

All students and staff of the College are entitled to register as members of the Library and Resource Centre and to use the facilities for the purpose of their course of study or College employment. Membership may also be extended to other individuals for a small fee, and study access is available by arrangement for non-members.

Use of our facilities implies acceptance of the rules designed to protect the interests of all users and ensure the College operates efficiently and effectively.

Rules Governing the Use of Learning Resources

Access

- Membership is for educational and College purposes only.
- ID cards are required for access and are non-transferable.
- Non-members may gain study access by arrangement, following specific guidelines.

Use of facillties

- Personal belongings are the responsibility of the user; items left unattended may be removed.
- Silence must be maintained in designated ‘Silent’ areas.
- Eating and drinking are prohibited in public areas of the Library.

Health & Safety

- Personal equipment must meet Health & Safety standards for use with College mains supplies.
- Written evidence of compliance is required.

Did You Know?

Our Library hosts over 100,000 academic resources, including books, research papers, journals, and access to leading online databases. Explore our resource catalogue online or visit us in person for personalized support.
