Information for Students

Rules Governing the Use of the Library and Resource Centre

Introduction

All students and staff of the College are entitled to register as members of the Library and Resource Centre and to use the facilities for the purpose of their course of study or College employment. Membership may also be extended to other individuals and organisations: a fee is usually charged.

Rules

A. Access

  • The use of Library and Resource Centre facilities is normally limited to educational purposes.
  • All individuals wishing to become members must comply with registration procedures.
  • Non-members may be admitted for study purposes by arrangement.
  • ID cards issued by the College are non-transferable and must be shown upon request.

B. Use of Facilities

  • The Library is not responsible for personal belongings left unattended.
  • Users are required to maintain silence in designated areas.
  • Mobile phones must be turned off in all study areas.
  • Eating and drinking are not permitted in public areas.
